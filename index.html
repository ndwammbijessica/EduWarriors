About EduWarriors

We believe that quality tutoring should be both effective and accessible. Our goal is to help students build a strong understanding of their subjects, develop confidence in their abilities, and become academic warriors!

We offer online tutoring for Grade 10-12 students in Mathematics and English, with options for both personalised one-on-one lessons and structured group practice sessions. This allows us to support students with different learning needs, goals and schedules.

By delivering lessons online, we make it easier for students to receive high-quality academic support from the comfort of their own homes - without compromising structure, focus or results.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I book a free consultation?

You can book a free consultation by clicking the "Book a Free Consultation" button on the website and completing the short Google Form. Once the form is submitted, you'll be contacted to schedule a convenient time to discuss academic goals, challenges, and next steps.

2. How do online tutoring sessions work?

All lessons are conducted online in a live, interactive format. Sessions are structured to explain key concepts clearly, encourage questions, and provide guided practice. Depending on the type of session, lessons may be fully personalised or focused on shared practice and reinforcement.

3. Are lessons one-on-one or group-based?

Both options are available.

  • One-on-one sessions are fully personalised and tailored to the student's pace, learning style, and specific needs.
  • Group sessions focus on guided practice, problem-solving, and exam-style questions. These sessions are less personalised but are ideal for reinforcing concepts in a collaborative setting.
4. How do you track student progress?

Progress is monitored through ongoing practice, feedback during lessons, and regular evaluation of understanding. Areas of difficulty are identified early, allowing lesson content to be adjusted to support consistent and long-term academic improvement.

5. How often should a student attend sessions?

Most students benefit from one to two sessions per week, depending on their academic goals and workload. During the free consultation, a personalised recommendation is made to ensure the most effective learning plan.

6. What platform do you use for lessons?

Lessons are primarily conducted using Google Meet. However, alternative platforms can be used if a student specifically requests them. All that is required is a stable internet connection and a device with audio and video capabilities.

7. Can sessions be rescheduled?

Yes. Sessions may be rescheduled with prior notice. Flexibility is offered to accommodate school commitments, examinations, and unforeseen circumstances, provided sufficient notice is given.

8. How much does tutoring cost?
  • One-on-one tutoring: R115 per hour
  • Group sessions: R250 per month

Full details, including recommendations on which option may be most suitable, are discussed during the free consultation.
